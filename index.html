About Me

I am pursuing a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at PCCoE, Pune. I am driven by a deep fascination with the world of semiconductors and its pivotal role in modern technology, particularly captivated by VLSI (Very Large-Scale Integration) and the entire process of chip creation. My interest spans from the intricacies of semiconductor fabrication to the critical steps of packaging the finished integrated circuit. What truly excites me is the challenge of chip designing, translating complex concepts into functional, physical layouts. Furthermore, I am eager to pioneer the integration of generative AI into the field of electronics, utilizing its power to revolutionize and optimize VLSI design methodologies and accelerate innovation in the next generation of silicon technology.
